Privacy Policy – Monroe County Court Records

At Monroe County Court Records, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains the types of data we collect, how we use it, and the steps we take to keep it secure. Our goal is to make sure you clearly understand how your personal information is handled whenever you access our services.

We want you to feel confident using our platform to search and access Monroe County court records. By being transparent about our privacy practices, we aim to give you the assurance that your information is collected and managed responsibly. If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you may contact us anytime at info@monroecountycourt.org.

Information We Collect

When you use Monroe County Court Records, information may be collected in different ways. Some information is provided directly by you, such as when you contact us by email or through our website. This may include your name, email address, and the details you choose to share. Other information is gathered automatically while you browse our site. This includes your IP address, browser type, device type, operating system, and the way you use our website, such as pages visited and time spent on each page. We also use cookies and similar technologies to remember your preferences and improve the browsing experience. These small files are stored on your device and help us understand how you interact with our site.

How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used for multiple purposes. We always aim to use data in ways that improve security, provide services efficiently, and enhance your overall experience on our site.

We may use your information to:

  • Respond to your questions or requests.
  • Provide access to Monroe County public court records.
  • Improve website performance and user experience.
  • Monitor traffic, detect unusual activity, and maintain security.
  • Communicate with you about updates, services, or inquiries.

Disclosure of Information

We respect your privacy and do not sell or trade your pers o nal information to third parties. Your information may only be shared in limited circumstances. For example, we may be required by law, subpoena, or court order to provide certain details. In addition, we may rely on trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our website, hosting data, or improving security. These service providers are expected to maintain confidentiality and safeguard your information. Sharing may also occur if necessary to protect against fraud, security threats, or other harmful activities.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies to enhance functionality and performance. Cookies allow us to remember your settings, improve site speed, and understand visitor patterns. Temporary session cookies are deleted after you close your browser, while persistent cookies may remain until you remove them manually. We may also use analytics tools that rely on cookies to evaluate how visitors interact with our site. You can adjust your browser settings to disable cookies, but please note that certain features of the site may not function properly without them.

Security of Data

Protecting the information we collect is an important responsibility. At Monroe County Court Records, we use a combination of technical and organizational measures such as encryption, firewalls, and restricted access to data. These measures are intended to reduce the risk of unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. While we strive to protect personal information, no method of online transmission can be guaranteed as completely secure. For this reason, we encourage users to exercise caution when sharing personal details online.

Access to Court Records

The primary service we provide is access to public court records from Monroe County. These records are public documents that may include details about legal proceedings, judgments, or filings. By law, such records are considered public information. We make them accessible in a convenient way, but we are not responsible for how others use this information once obtained. Users are expected to follow all applicable laws when requesting or using court records.

Third-Party Links

Our website may include links to third-party websites for informational purposes. These external sites are not managed by Monroe County Court Records, and we cannot guarantee their content or privacy practices. Before providing any personal information on an external site, we encourage you to read their privacy policy. Our policy applies only to information collected directly through https://monroecountycourt.org .

Children’s Privacy

Our services are intended for adults and are not designed for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that data has been provided by a child, we will take steps to remove it promptly. Parents or guardians who believe their child may have submitted personal details should contact us immediately at info@monroecountycourt.org.

Retention of Information

We only keep personal information for as long as it is needed to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy. When information is no longer required, it is securely deleted or anonymized. Retention times may vary depending on legal obligations, technical requirements, or the type of data collected.

Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights related to your personal information. These rights may allow you to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you.
  • Ask for corrections or updates to inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your personal information where applicable.
  • Opt out of cookies or specific types of data collection.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at info@monroecountycourt.org.

Changes to Our Privacy Policy

From time to time, we may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our services, technologies, or legal requirements. Updates will be posted on this page along with the date of the most recent revision. We encourage visitors to review this page regularly to remain informed about our practices.
